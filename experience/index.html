Research Project

Wearable Knee Cap Microwave Sensor for Osteoarthritis Detection

Feb 2023 - Jun 2023

In response to the significant global public health concern posed by osteoarthritis (OA), particularly in aging populations and amid rising obesity rates, there is a growing demand for innovative solutions.

In our study, we focused on designing a highly efficient monopole antenna embedded within a flexible nylon substrate fabric renowned for its exceptional elasticity. Through meticulous design and numerical simulation, we optimized the antenna's performance, specifically targeting operation at 2.45 GHz within the Industrial, Scientific, and Medical (ISM) band.

Furthermore, we constructed a human knee model to investigate the resonance frequency effects of healthy and osteoarthritic knees. By analyzing changes in the antenna's peak resonating frequency, our aim is to offer a non-invasive method for diagnosing knee osteoarthritis. This research represents a step forward in leveraging wearable technology to address pressing healthcare challenges.
